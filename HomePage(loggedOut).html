What is CookPal?

CookPal is a web application that makes cooking easier by allowing users to record, categorize, and share recipes as well as organize and plan future meals.

By storing your recipes in CookPal, you can rest easy knowing that they are secure and can be accessed by browser anytime.

Key Features

  Storing Recipes

We know that keeping copies of recipes can get messy with recipes getting lost or damaged.

CookPal allows you to record recipes and securely store them in our database and is accessible anywhere with internet.

  Custom Categories

Create your own categories and use them to organize your recipes.

  Sharing Recipes

Being able to share your recipes is almost as important as being able to make it.

Share your recipes in CookPal with just a click of a button.

  Plan meals

Plan what meals you want to eat throughout the upcoming week.
